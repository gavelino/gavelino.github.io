Enrollment is now open for the **Object-Oriented Programming (POO)** course for the **2025/1 semester** at UFMG.

---

## About the Course

This course introduces the fundamental principles of object-oriented programming using Java. Topics include:

- Classes, objects, and encapsulation
- Inheritance and polymorphism
- Interfaces and abstract classes
- Design patterns and best practices
- Unit testing with JUnit

## Schedule

- **Semester:** 2025/1 (March – July 2025)
- **Location:** Department of Computer Science, UFMG
- **Instructor:** Prof. Guilherme Avelino

## Enrollment

Students interested in enrolling should do so through the official UFMG academic system. For questions, feel free to send an email.
